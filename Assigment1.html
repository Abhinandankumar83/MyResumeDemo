Few Facts About The World We Live In

Earth, our home, is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to harbor life. It's a dynamic, rocky planet with a diverse surface featuring mountains, valleys, plains, and most notably, oceans, which cover about 70% of its surface. Earth is also unique for having liquid water on its surface and an atmosphere with a mix of gases, including oxygen, which is essential for life as we know it
